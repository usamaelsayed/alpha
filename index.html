Who Are We?

One of the major companies in the field of producing and supplying all types of interior, exterior and anti-explosion lighting, street lighting units, factories, playgrounds, facades, garden lighting and decorative lighting units in addition to directed lighting units in cooperation with major .international companies

Due to the industrial and urban expansion in Egypt, demand for lighting products is soaring. With the new requirements for energy saving, LED lighting is currently the no. 1 choice for both suppliers and consumers due to its high efficacy and excellent lighting properties. ALFA provides a variety of LED lighting products which fulfill every purpose and field (urban/residential/administrative/industrial). ALFA also provides lighting design service so it is your one-stop-shop for lighting solutions
